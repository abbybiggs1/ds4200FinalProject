DS4200 Final Project

Key Takeaways

What our analysis of consumer shopping behavior reveals — and what it means for retailers.

📦

Inventory

Stock up on inventories during the prior season to meet consumer demand
before peak purchase periods.

📍

Regional Strategy

Push marketing strategies featuring pricier items in states with high median purchase
amounts — particularly Alaska, Pennsylvania, and Arizona.

💳

Payments

Offer all types of payment methods across all age ranges to reduce friction at checkout.

👥

Demographics

Push marketing toward women, who are currently purchasing less despite being a
large segment of the customer base.

👗

Category Focus

Prioritize stocking clothing, as it consistently represents the highest spend category
across demographics.
